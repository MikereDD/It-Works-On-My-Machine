# v5.7 — Mention & Inline Intelligence Layer

## Overview

v5.7 introduces Raziel’s first conversational mention layer.

Raziel can now respond naturally inside group chats using:
- mention-style commands
- inline operational requests
- conversational utility interactions

without requiring traditional slash commands.

This marks the beginning of Raziel evolving from:
- a media downloader
into:
- an operational Telegram assistant
