# v5.4.8 — Clip Metadata & Upload Caption Polish

## Overview

v5.4.8 improves the `/clip` workflow by adding visible clip timing
metadata throughout the entire pipeline.

Raziel now displays:
- clip start/end range
- calculated clip duration
- normalized timestamps

during:
- queue confirmation
- active clipping status
- final upload captions

This makes clipped uploads significantly more informative and polished
inside Telegram chats.
